Atal pension scheme

Atal Pension Yojana (APY) is an old age income security scheme for a savings account holder in the age group of 18-40 years who is not an income tax-payee. The scheme helps in addressing the longevity risks among the workers in the unorganized sector and encourages the workers to voluntarily save for their retirement.

Swachh Bharat

Swachh Bharat or Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Campaign Clean India) is a national level campaign by the Government of India covering 4041 statutory towns to clean the streets, roads and infrastructure of the country.

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Scheme

The PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana is the best welfare scheme launched by the Indian government. It is a scheme for farmers of our country. PM Kisan government scheme pays Rs 6000 every year to eligible farmers. To get benefits of this scheme, around ten crore farmers are also enrolled in it.

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan scheme

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) is National Mission for Financial Inclusion to ensure access to financial services, namely, Banking/ Savings & Deposit Accounts, Remittance, Credit, Insurance, Pension in an affordable manner. Objectives: Ensure access of financial products & services at an affordable cost.
